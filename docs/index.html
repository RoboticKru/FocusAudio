Features

Built for multitaskers

Whether you're watching lectures while listening to music, or juggling calls and videos — FocusAudio keeps your ears focused.

🎵

Smooth Crossfading

Audio gracefully fades in and out over 1.2 seconds instead of jarring, instant muting. Feels natural and seamless.

⏸

Fade Then Pause

Enable "Fade then pause" mode to silently fade background audio to zero, then pause it — so your track never skips or loses its position.

🧠

Smart Fallback

Paused your lecture? FocusAudio detects the silence and automatically fades your background music back in, hands-free.

🎚️

Per-App Volume Mixer

A sleek popup mixer lets you set exact volume levels and roles for every app — Main, Background, or ignored entirely.

🌐

Multi-Browser Aware

Intelligently handles Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Brave, Opera, and Vivaldi — matching audio sessions to active tabs by title.

📌

System Tray

Runs silently in your system tray. Toggle on and off or quit with a simple right-click. Zero desktop clutter.

🛡️

Role-Based Control

Assign apps as Main, Background, or Ignore. Background apps duck or pause when a Main app is in focus — fully customisable.

⚡

Lightweight

Tiny footprint. No background services, no telemetry, no internet required. Just a single portable .exe file.

How It Works

Up and running in seconds

No installation wizards, no configuration files, no admin permissions needed.

1

Download FocusAudio.exe

Grab the single portable executable from GitHub Releases. No Python or dependencies needed.

2

Double-click to run

FocusAudio launches silently into your system tray. No windows, no clutter. You'll see a small green icon near your clock.

3

Just use your computer

Switch between apps as you normally would. FocusAudio automatically fades audio in and out based on which window is active. Enjoy the focus.
